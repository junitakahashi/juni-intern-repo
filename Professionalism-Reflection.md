## Have you ever experienced or witnessed great teamwork and professionalism? What made it effective?
Yes. When I was working as a marketing intern at an inbound travel marketing agency, I experienced excellent teamwork and professionalism. Most communication took place on Slack, with occasional Google Meet check-ins. Team members responded promptly to each other, which kept the workflow smooth and transparent. What stood out most was the openness of the environment where people felt comfortable asking questions, raising concerns, and sharing ideas without hesitation. I also had fortnightly 1-on-1 meetings with my manager, which provided consistent feedback, recognition, and support. This transparency and psychological safety helped everyone stay aligned and motivated.
