## What do you think are the biggest benefits and challenges of Agile?

Benefits:

- Agile allows teams to adapt quickly when priorities or user needs change.
- Continuous feedback leads to better decisions and improved product quality.
- Breaking tasks into smaller pieces makes work less overwhelming and easier to track.
- Collaboration is stronger because communication happens frequently.

Challenges:

- Constant change can feel chaotic if not documented or communicated clearly.
- It requires discipline to keep tasks small and maintain transparency.
- For new team members, the lack of rigid structure can feel confusing at first.
- Miscommunication may lead to complicated issues in the team project.
